BUSINESSS MODEL

VALUE PROPOSITION

Fresh Cut Butchery operates by providing fresh and high-quality pork, chicken, and milk to meet the needs of both households and businesses. The business focuses on delivering value to customers by offering affordable prices, hygienically prepared products, and reliable service. By ensuring freshness and quality, the business builds trust and satisfaction among its customers.

TARGET CUSTOMERS

The target market of Fresh Cut Butchery includes local households, restaurants, hotels, and food vendors who require a consistent supply of fresh meat and dairy products. These customers depend on the business for daily consumption and bulk purchases, making it important to maintain quality and availability at all times.

PRODUCTS

The business generates revenue mainly through direct sales of pork, chicken, and milk to individual customers, as well as bulk sales to businesses such as restaurants and catering services. This allows the business to earn income while serving different types of customers.

KEY ACTIVITIES

Fresh Cut Butchery carries out key activities such as sourcing products from trusted suppliers, cutting and preparing meat, packaging, and selling products to customers. Maintaining cleanliness and hygiene is also a major part of daily operations to ensure food safety.

KEY RESOURCES

The business relies on important resources such as skilled workers, equipment like freezers and cutting tools, a physical shop location, and a steady supply of products. These resources are essential for smooth operation and service delivery.

COST STRUCTURE

The cost structure of Fresh Cut Butchery includes all the expenses required to run the business efficiently on a daily basis. The major costs involve purchasing stock such as pork, chicken, and milk from suppliers, which forms the largest part of the business expenses. In addition, the business incurs costs for employee salaries, especially for butchers and shop assistants who handle operations and customer service. Other important costs include electricity for running refrigerators and freezers to keep products fresh, rent for the shop location, and transportation costs for delivering products from suppliers. Proper management of these costs is essential to ensure profitability and long-term sustainability of the business.

KEY PARTNERS

Fresh Cut Butchery works with key partners including local farmers, suppliers, and transport providers who help in delivering fresh products on time. These partnerships ensure that the business can maintain consistent quality and supply.
