INTRODUCTION

Fresh Cut Butchery is a startup business established to provide fresh and high-quality pork, chicken, and milk to the local community. The business was created to address the need for reliable and hygienically prepared meat and dairy products.The idea of the business came from observing that many customers struggle to find trusted suppliers of fresh food products. Fresh Cut Butchery aims to solve this problem by ensuring consistent quality, affordability, and excellent customer service.

OUR MISSION

To provide fresh, safe, and high-quality meat and dairy products while maintaining excellent customer service and hygiene standards.

OUR VISION

To become a leading and trusted supplier of pork, chicken, and milk in the local market.

OUR GOALS

  • To deliver fresh and affordable products
  • To build strong customer relationships
  • To support local farmers and suppliers

!!!Fresh Cut Butchery is committed to growing its business while contributing positively to the community and local economy.!!!
